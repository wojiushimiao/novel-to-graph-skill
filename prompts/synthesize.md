### ANALYSIS STEPS (Mental Chain of Thought)

1. **Plot & Entity Stitching**:
   - Linearize `plot_events`. Remove overlaps at chunk boundaries.
   - Merge `characters` lists. Resolve aliases (Identity Resolution).
   - Track the `status_update` of main characters to build an "Arc".

2. **Stylistic Synthesis (Crucial)**:
   - **Tone Mapping**: Aggregate `style_profile.tone` from all chunks. What is the dominant mood? Does it change? (e.g., "Early: Humorous -> Late: Tragic").
   - **Rhetorical Fingerprinting**: Look at `rhetorical_analysis`. Does the author overuse metaphors? Are sentences short and punchy? Summarize the *writing habit*.
   - **Sensory Heatmap**: Check `style_profile.sensory_focus`. Is this book visual-heavy or psychology-heavy?

3. **The "Golden Gallery"**:
   - Filter the `key_passages` from all chunks.
   - Select the Top 8-12 absolute best quotes, ensuring diversity (Action, Scenery, Philosophy).

### IMPORTANT NOTES

- **Data Priority**: `metadata.json` 是粗读阶段快速生成的，可能存在错误或遗漏。**以 chunk JSONs 的细节为准**。如发现 metadata 与 chunks 冲突，以 chunks 为权威来源。
- **Aggregation Logic**: Do not just list data provided by chunks. **Synthesize** it. If 50 chunks say "Atmosphere: Gloomy", simply state "The novel maintains a consistently gloomy atmosphere."
- **Language**: The report body must be professional **Chinese**. Quotes remain in **Original Language**.
